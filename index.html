Ordinance No. 1

This ordinance focuses on the segregation of waste at the source. Residents are required to separate biodegradable, non-biodegradable, and recyclable materials.

Ordinance No. 2

This ordinance mandates community cleanup drives every month to ensure that public spaces remain clean and free of waste.

Ordinance No. 3

This ordinance establishes recycling facilities in various barangays to encourage the recycling of materials and reduce landfill waste.

Ordinance No. 4

This ordinance promotes the use of composting for biodegradable waste to create organic fertilizers for urban gardening.

Ordinance No. 5

This ordinance regulates the schedule of waste collection to ensure timely and efficient waste management services.
